# Habibi's
Habibi's Diner is a breakfast and lunch diner in [Salwa](../Salwa.md) where [Sam](../../Person/Sam.md) and [Zach](../../Person/Zach.md) met each other, and later where they both met [Musa](../../Person/Musa.md).

# Emira
Emira the info broker (they/them) is a cat, friend of [Zach](wiki/Person/Zach.md) by way of a mutual friend [Harshul](wiki/Person/Harshul.md) and information source for [Aria](wiki/Person/Aria.md) and [Miriam](wiki/Person/Miriam.md).

When [Harshul](wiki/Person/Harshul.md) told [Zach](wiki/Person/Zach.md) to come to them for a lead on a flat, they sent [Zach](wiki/Person/Zach.md) to [Nithiya](wiki/Person/Nithiya.md), who they are friends with.

Hangs around at [Nyah's Shisha](wiki/Location/Commercial/Nyahs%20Shisha.md). Emira is a [Rasul](wiki/Person/Group/Rasul.md), along with their friend, also a cat, [Omar](wiki/Omar).

Emira overheard information about [Zhen](wiki/Person/Zhen.md) from Miriam and [Musa](wiki/Person/Musa.md) at [Nya-Nyails](wiki/Location/Commercial/Nya-Nyails.md), which [Aria](wiki/Person/Aria.md) told them to eventually leak to the media, partially in order to deter Miriam from looking for Zhen.

[Sam](wiki/Person/Sam.md) calls Emira 'greasy cat'.

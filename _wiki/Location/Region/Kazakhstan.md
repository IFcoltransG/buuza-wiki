# Kazakhstan
Kazakhstan is the northernmost region of [Dawlat Al-Harir](wiki/Location/Dawlat%20Al-Harir.md). [Sam](wiki/Person/Sam.md) has lived here. [Zarina](wiki/Person/Zarina.md) is representative, and she lives there with [Sam's mother](wiki/Person/Sams%20mother.md). Holds special festivities for [Al Basir](wiki/Culture/Deity/Al%20Basir.md).

Borders [Eastern Turkistan](wiki/Location/Region/Eastern%20Turkistan.md) and [Western Turkistan](wiki/Location/Region/Western%20Turkistan.md) to the south.
